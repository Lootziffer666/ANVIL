# FLOW Family

The FLOW family includes LOOM, FLOW, SPIN, and SMASH.

## Role In ANVIL

- LOOM — shared structural engine / relation layer
- FLOW — deterministic orthographic and near-surface normalization
- SPIN — writing and sentence-structure workshop
- SMASH — micro-intervention layer for writing blocks

## ANVIL Relationship

ANVIL can hold shared development rules, corpus provenance discipline, prompt templates, and global knownbugs.

FLOW-family language logic remains project-local unless a concrete shared module is proven.
